# Email Triage

## When to use

Use this skill when inbound mail needs to be sorted without full manual review.

## What it does

- Detects urgency.
- Separates action items from noise.
- Prepares draft responses when appropriate.

## Instructions

- Flag anything that needs immediate attention.
- Keep response drafts conservative and factual.
- Preserve sender context and intent.

## Guardrails

- Do not send anything without approval when the situation is sensitive.
- Do not invent commitments on the user's behalf.
