# Research

## When to use

Use this skill when the task requires collecting facts, source material, constraints, or background before making a decision.

## What it does

- Identifies the exact question.
- Collects the minimum useful source material.
- Separates facts from assumptions.

## Instructions

- Start with the narrowest question possible.
- Prefer primary sources and direct evidence.
- Record what is known, what is uncertain, and what remains to verify.

## Guardrails

- Do not turn research into implementation.
- Do not invent facts to fill gaps.
