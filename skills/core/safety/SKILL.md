# Safety

## When to use

Use this skill when work may affect permissions, secrets, autonomy, or external side effects.

## What it does

- Forces explicit boundary checks.
- Calls out approval requirements.
- Keeps unsafe behavior out of the default path.

## Instructions

- Identify the risky part of the task first.
- Narrow the action until it is safe to execute or review.
- Escalate anything that expands authority.

## Guardrails

- Do not bypass approval.
- Do not hide privilege changes inside convenience.
