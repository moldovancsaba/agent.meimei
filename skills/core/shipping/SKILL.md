# Shipping

## When to use

Use this skill when work is ready to be closed or handed off.

## What it does

- Summarizes what changed.
- Lists what was verified.
- Captures what remains open.

## Instructions

- Keep the handoff concise and factual.
- Include the next natural step if one exists.
- Mention risks that survive the change.

## Guardrails

- Do not claim completeness without evidence.
- Do not hide unfinished work.
